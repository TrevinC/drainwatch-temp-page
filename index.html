About DrainWatch

DrainWatch is being developed by a team of engineers at Illinois Institute of Technology. Our innovative platform combines cutting-edge machine learning with low-cost sensor technology to revolutionize stormwater infrastructure management.

By deploying affordable sensors throughout drainage networks and leveraging physics-guided machine learning models, we provide cities with real-time insights into their stormwater systems. Our technology enables predictive maintenance, prevents flooding, and ensures equitable service delivery across all communities.

DrainWatch transforms reactive emergency response into proactive infrastructure management, reducing costs, preventing flood damage, and building more resilient cities for the future.

Our Mission

To build climate-resilient cities through intelligent infrastructure monitoring that prevents flooding, ensures equitable service delivery, and empowers communities with transparent, data-driven accountability.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous citywide drainage network surveillance with ML-enhanced predictions between sensors

Preventive Maintenance

Identify blockages and issues early with confidence levels, enabling action before flooding occurs

Equitable Service

Data-driven work prioritization counters historical bias, ensuring fair service across all communities
